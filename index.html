Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping from Ghana take?

Ocean freight to Europe typically takes 18–28 days. Air freight is 3–7 days. Exact timing depends on route, customs, and cargo type — we give you an ETA at booking.

What documents do I need to import a vehicle?

You generally need a valid ID, purchase invoice, bill of lading, import permit, and customs declaration. We guide you through every document and handle port clearance.

Do you offer cargo insurance?

Yes. All shipments can include marine or transit insurance. We recommend it for high-value goods, vehicles, and electronics.

How do I pay for shipping?

We accept bank transfer, mobile money, and card payments. A deposit secures your booking; balance is due before release at destination. See our payments page.

Can I track my container in real time?

Yes. Use your tracking or container number on our Track page to see vessel location, port status, and delivery progress.
