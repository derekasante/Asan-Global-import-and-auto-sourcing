Frequently Asked Questions

How long does vehicle import take?

Vehicle import typically takes 25–45 days depending on the origin country, shipping method, and customs processing. We provide accurate ETAs once we have your vehicle details.

What documents do I need to import a vehicle?

We handle all required documentation including purchase invoice, bill of lading, export certificate, import permit, and customs declaration. Our staff guides you through the entire process.

How does your staff manage my import?

Each import is assigned a dedicated staff member who coordinates sourcing, documentation, shipping arrangements, customs clearance, and keeps you updated throughout the process.

What import fees should I expect?

Import fees include our service fee, customs duties, shipping costs, and documentation fees. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden costs before you commit.

Can I track my vehicle import in real time?

Yes. Use your import tracking number on our tracking page to see real-time status updates, documentation progress, and delivery milestones.
